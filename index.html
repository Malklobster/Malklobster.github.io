Why dogs are good companions

There have been multiple studies about how dogs are good companions, They improve your heart health keep you fit and active and help you lose weight. They also reduce stress improve social life and even help with depression! Its also nice just to have a furry friend to snuggle with when your sad.

How dogs help humans

Dogs have evolved over the years due to what humans breed them to do, to this day humans still use dogs to help with hearding, hunting, and even medical related things. Recent studies show that dogs a capable of smelling certain types of cancer and are soon going to use dogs in hospitals. Besides being able to help the medical side of things they can also help with anxiety and keep patients calm and happy.
